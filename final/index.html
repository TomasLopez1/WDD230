Carlsbad, US:

Coastal city founded in 1952, located in northern San Diego County, California, United States. Carlsbad is a city with a low population density and a high educational level in its school district, the city is well known for its high quality of life. A major concern of this city is environmental stewardship. The city has enacted ordinances that protect wildlife habitat, one of the first ordinances in the state of California. In addition, the city is committed to preserving its lands.
