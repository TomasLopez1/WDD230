About Us

Bountifoods is a company specializing in locally grown organic food. We are physically located in Carlsbad, California. Our company sells fruits and produce directly from local farmers, although we also use some national and international suppliers. We handle orders over the phone and through this website. Our mission is to provide healthy, fresh, freshly prepared food options. We know you will love our delicious and nutritious products, enjoy!
